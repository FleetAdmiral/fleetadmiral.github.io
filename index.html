Why Not?

WhyNot? is a collective of Data Engineers and Scientists/Researchers - each with expertise in at least one domain of Machine/Deep Learning. We are a passionate group that weld our knowledge to solve tough problems - be it the kind that derives nature of data (Eg: Anamoly Detection, Pattern recognition), or the kind that helps generate data (Eg: Prediction, Simulation, etc). An in-house collective, we're all graduates from IIIT-Hyderabad - India's premier research institute. So rest assured, not only would you be working with some of the brightest minds in India, you can also be sure that we keep ourselves up-to-date and use industry standard methods to solve your issue - no matter what the domain or task! We assure you, your data is in good hands!

What We Can Do for You

Natural Language Processing

Coming straight out of Asia's largest research lab for linguistics (LTRC), there is no task - from Sentiment Analysis to Multi-lingual Chatbots - that's too tough for our experts.

Anamoly detection

With years of expertise, our Anamoly detection team can help provide an end-to-end solution: from identification of anamolies in your data, right to suggesting custom solutions for the same.

Data Analytics

Our expertise in prediction algorithms, pattern recognition and Deep Learning for various tasks allows us to be very competent to be able crunch any data you have - providing you with stats, patterns, person profile, predicted returns and any other features suitable to the problem.

Machine Learning

If you have any Data Science/Deep Learning problem that you'd like to tackle - no matter the domain - we'll build a custom team of members corresponding to your problem, and tackle it with proficiency. (Eg: We have, in the past, successfully delivered on problems in the automobile and also medical field).
